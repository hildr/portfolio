ABOUT

I’m graphic designer and photographer from Ukraine, studding for Web Design in Auckland, New Zealand. I found this job position on internet search and I’m really interested in this position. This position is a great opportunity for me to increase my skills, to increase myself as a designer and also this is great opportunity for my career.
I graduated Bachelor Degree in Graphic Design and Photography from University of East London in 2014. Also I was study Graphic Design and Digital Media during two and half year in University Teknologi Mara in Yemen. Also I have small experience in working as graphic designer. I had an internship in UNDP Health Support in South Africa, where I design marketing materials for UNDP Support to the Health Sector in Limpopo. Also I have some experience in working as freelancer, where I have some experiences in design and photo retouching.
I have skills in working with Adobe Creative Suite (designing) and Microsoft Office. I have skills in photo shooting and photo retouching, in 2D and 3D animation, in researching and preparing work before design. During my study I learned how to work for different types of campaign: advertising or social. I’m hardworking person and very punctual. I can work individually and in the team. I also have knowledge of HTML, CSS, Java Script, JQuery, Sass and building of web sites in Wordpress.
