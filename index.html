ABOUT ME

My name is Maria Bocharova. I'm a young Web and Graphic Designer from Ukraine, who's making their first steps in the design world.
I have a degree in Graphic Design and Photography from the University of East London in Malaysia. As a Web Designer I have completed ACG Yoobee School of Design school's web bootcamp in New Zealand.
I have work experience in Graphic Design field. I have been an intern designer in UNDP Health Support in South Africa and I have experience as a freelancer.
Design is my passion, this is my way to comunicate with the world. As a web designer I love to accept challenges and create something unique.
I'm open for any job opportunities.
